Connection Help

If the transfer doesn't start, try these steps:

  • Use a Mobile Hotspot: This is the most reliable way. Connect the PC to the phone's hotspot.
  • Check Wi-Fi Type: Ensure the network is set to "Private" or "Home" in your PC settings.
  • Disable VPN: VPNs often block local P2P traffic.
  • Disable Browser Extensions: Ad-blockers sometimes interfere with WebRTC.
  • Same Network: Ensure both devices are on the exact same Wi-Fi SSID.
